Instructions

Dither & NS Benchmark Tool is a website designed to provide the results of applying dither and/or noise shaping to low-level signals. Tests were performed on two signals:

1) Sine: Sinusoidal at997Hz, 2LSB (Least Significant Bit) peak level at 44.1kHz/24-bit.
2) Instrument: -86dBFS RMS instrument signal at 44.1kHz/24-bit.

Both signals were converted from 24-bit to 16-bit depth through dithering and/or noise shaping processing, using the plugins or DAWs indicated in each case.

Except for the gain application (after processed signal), no other processes, effects, or signal alterations were included in the measurement.
The plugins and DAWs used, along with their corresponding versions, are listed below:

Izotope Ozone v11 2.0.3296
FabFilter ProL2 2.21
Waves L2 14.12.90.381
Voxengo Elephant 5.1
DMG Limitless 1.19
ProTools Maxim/Dither 2024.10.0
Reaper 7.34
Ableton Live 11.3.13
